Tourist Attractions

Vigan Heritage Village

A UNESCO site with preserved Spanish colonial architecture. It is known for it's cobblestone streets, centuries- old stone houses, and a mix of Spanish and Filipino architectural styles that take visitors back to the colonial era.

Bantay Bell Tower

Bantay Bell Tower is located in Ilocos, Sur.Built in 1591, it orignally served as both a watchtower to guard againts pirates and as a bell tower for the nearby St. Augustine Church.

Bago Resort

This breath-taking and relaxing resort is located in Salcedo, Ilocos Sur. This is the first man made pool with natural spring water in the Province of Ilocos Sur.
